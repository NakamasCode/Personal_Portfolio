About Me

Hi, I’m Dedoyin Timi — an Electrical and Electronics Engineer with a strong passion for solving problems through technology, creativity, and design. My journey blends technical expertise with a drive to build practical solutions that people can use every day.

Over time, I’ve gained hands-on experience in automation systems, project engineering, and product design. Beyond engineering, I explore creative ventures such as shoemaking with my brand JohnTimeless, where craftsmanship meets timeless design. I also enjoy making music, especially playing the saxophone, which fuels my creativity and attention to detail.

My goal is simple — to keep learning, creating, and contributing to projects that make life easier, more efficient, and more inspiring. Whether it’s designing systems, building apps, or crafting shoes, I’m always committed to excellence, precision, and growth.
